Whereabouts

  • From Cagliari (Elmas) Airport, there is a regional train every 10 minutes that takes you to the center of Cagliari in about 7 minutes.

    In Cagliari, every 30 minutes, you can take the PQ bus line at Piazza Matteotti, in front of ARST, heading towards Quartu, with a stop at Golfo di Quartu S.E (Lido Mediterraneo) (approximately a 25-minute ride).

    From there, it's a 4-minute walk to Frontemare.

  • From the city center of Cagliari to the city center of Quartu Sant'Elena, there is the QEX bus line approximately every 10 minutes.

  • From the city center of Quartu, approximately every 10 minutes, there are several buses that will take you to Frontemare in about 30 minutes.
