We Are Getting Married June 8, 2024

Simona Pilloni

Simona is a great listener and a wonderful life companion. She fights for what she believes in and puts passion into every aspect of her life. She loves to travel, dreaming of all the places she would like to visit, but she also recharges by being with her friends and family. Simona is the best friend you could ask for, and she'll always be there to support you (and apparently, deal with Davide too...).

Davide Sellitri

Davide is a caring, kind and funny man who loves making people happy and taking care of his family and friends. Davide's unique approach to expressing his care for others involves preparing delicious meals, crafting extraordinary focacce and panzerotti, or creating special dishes that leave everyone content and satisfied. His remarkable commitment to ensuring everyone's happiness and delivering the finest is truly awe-inspiring. However, be cautious, as getting too near him might result in a weight gain of 2-3kg!

Our Sweet Story

First Time We Meet

8 Jan 2017

On a cold January evening in Kinsale, we crossed paths with a plan to be roomies and hunt for a place together. Who would've guessed that in our house-hunting adventure, we'd actually stumble upon something even better – a connection that felt like finding our soulmate? From there, our story played out like a movie, a love story made for the big screen!

Our First Date

1 April 2017

Let's pretend and say we actually went on a date! In our super cozy love nest, our love-life was getting kind of serious. We cooked up delicious dinners, caught awesome movies, and jammed to live music all over Cork city. And guess what? What we initially saw as our major differences ended up being our most powerful strengths, weaving together an utterly unique story that's all our own.

She Said Yes!

27 December 2022

The super romantic proposal Davide had been planning for so long turned into a total comedy at Milan Airport. Simona was the only one who could crack up laughing at Davide's Elton John-inspired proposal, totally thinking he was just kidding around (which he does a lot!), especially when he pulled out a paper handmade ring! It took some extra effort to make things serious, but then she finally caught on that he was for real and saying "Will you marry me?" Amidst all the laughter, she managed to say "Yes!"
